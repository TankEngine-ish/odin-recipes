Arden Apple Pie

Description

Although there are an abundance of exotic fruits to be found in Ardenweald,

their variety of humble apples is the best I've ever tasted.

Ingredients

  • flour
  • oats
  • brown sugar
  • apples
  • unsalted butter
  • lemon juice
  • raisins

    • Steps

    1. Combine the dry ingredients in the bowl of a food processor and pulse a few times to combine.
    2. Add the butter, and pulse until there are no pieces larger than a pea.
    3. Pulse a few more times while drizzling the water in, until the mixture just forms a ball.
